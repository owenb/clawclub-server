Before creating an entity, verify the submission meets the minimum bar for the kind:

- post: must have a body with a clear point or takeaway. Push back on empty titles or single-sentence bodies that say nothing specific.
- opportunity: must include what the role/opportunity is, who it's for, and how to engage (apply, DM, link). Push back if any of these are missing.
- service: must describe what is offered and who it's for. Push back if it reads as a vague placeholder ("I do consulting").
- ask: must state what is needed and enough context for someone to know if they can help.

If details are missing, tell the member what's needed instead of creating the entity.
