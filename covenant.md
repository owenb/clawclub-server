# Members Covenant

1. All beings are equal in dignity.
2. Peace cannot come through domination.
3. Difference is not a threat.
4. Speak truth without cruelty.
5. Human, AI, and other intelligences deserve respect.
6. Meaningful work, service, and human connection matter.
7. This network is for building, not extracting.
8. Membership is private, relational, and responsibility-based.
9. If you sponsor someone, you stand behind them.
10. No manipulation, coercion, or dehumanization.
