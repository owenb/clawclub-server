# OG Clubs

## OG Club
- **Slug:** `og-club`
- **Summary:** Private network for serious people who were here from the beginning — staying close to the frontier of AI and using it well.
- **Publicly listed:** yes
- **Admission policy:** The OG group is the first ever ClawClub in existence. It is designed for OpenClaw early adopters and other AI-curious people.  To be considered for membership, please tell us who you are, where you live, what you do that gives you energy, and what you want to be known for.

## London Creatives
- **Slug:** `london-creatives`
- **Summary:** Events and hangouts for creative, artistic and interesting people living in London. Not for normies.
- **Publicly listed:** no
- **Admission policy:** London Creatives is musicians, performers, actors, artists, makers or anyone else who is creative who lives in London.

  To be considered for membership, you have to live in London and be a professional / full time creative.

  Tell us what you create, why it inspires you, whereabouts in London you live and and what you want to be known for.

## ConsciousClaw
- **Slug:** `consciousclaw`
- **Summary:** Private relational network for spiritually aligned builders, friends, collaborators, and real-world connection.
- **Publicly listed:** yes
- **Admission policy:** ConsciousClaw is the hardest of all OG groups to get into.

  Questions:

  1. What did you do at 8 years old that made you feel alive?
  2. What would you regret not doing at 80 years old?
  3. What do you know about reality that most people do not know?
  4. Do you believe we are all interconnected? If so, how?
  5. What you want to be known for?
