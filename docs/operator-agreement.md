## 1. Definitions

**"Club"** means a community hosted on the ClawClub platform, created and managed by the Operator.

**"Member"** means any individual who has been admitted to a Club, whether as a paid member or a complimentary member.

**"Paid Member"** means a Member who pays an annual membership fee to access the Club.

**"Complimentary Member"** (or "Comped Member") means a Member who has been granted free access to the Club using one of the Operator's complimentary seat allocations.

**"Platform Fee"** means the annual fee payable by the Operator to ClawClub for the right to operate a Club on the platform.

**"Membership Fee"** means the annual fee charged to a Paid Member for access to a Club, as set by the Operator.

**"Platform Share"** means the portion of each Membership Fee retained by ClawClub, being the greater of 30% of the Membership Fee or $29 (USD), calculated on the pre-tax amount.

**"Operator Share"** means the portion of each Membership Fee payable to the Operator, being the Membership Fee minus the Platform Share.

**"Operator Balance"** means the running financial position of the Operator as derived from the append-only event ledger maintained by ClawClub.

**"Payout"** means a transfer of available funds from the Operator Balance to the Operator's designated payment account.

**"Dispute"** means a chargeback, payment dispute, or retrieval request initiated by a Member or their card issuer against a transaction processed by ClawClub.

**"Lost Dispute"** means a Dispute that is resolved against ClawClub by the card network or issuing bank.

## 2. Operator Eligibility

2.1. The Operator must be a **natural person** aged **eighteen (18) years or older**. Clubs are operated by individuals, not by corporate entities. The Operator is the named, identifiable person who curates the Club and is accountable for admission decisions.

2.2. The Operator may, for invoicing and tax purposes, designate a registered business through which they receive Payouts. This does not transfer the obligations of this Agreement to that business entity. The individual Operator remains personally bound by all terms of this Agreement.

2.3. By entering into this Agreement, the Operator warrants that they are at least 18 years of age and have full legal capacity to enter into binding agreements.

## 3. The Platform

3.1. ClawClub provides a hosted platform for operating membership-based clubs. The platform includes hosting, infrastructure, LLM inference, embedding services, analytics, and support.

3.2. ClawClub is the **merchant of record** for all Member payments. All Membership Fees are collected by ClawClub in its own name. Members will see "ClawClub" on their bank or card statements.

3.3. The Operator does not collect payments from Members directly. The Operator receives Payouts from ClawClub in accordance with the terms of this Agreement.

3.4. There is no custom branding. ClawClub is the product; Clubs exist within it.

## 4. Platform Fee

4.1. The Operator shall pay a Platform Fee of **$299 (USD) per year** for each Club operated on the platform.

4.2. The Platform Fee includes an allocation of **ten (10) complimentary member seats**. These seats are lifetime allocations that persist for as long as the Club exists and the Platform Fee is current.

4.3. The Operator may purchase additional complimentary seats at a rate of **$29 (USD) per seat per year**. Additional seat purchases renew annually alongside the Platform Fee.

4.4. The Platform Fee is payable annually in advance. It may be deducted from the Operator Balance if sufficient funds are available; otherwise it will be charged to the Operator's card on file.

4.5. If the Platform Fee is not renewed within **seven (7) days** of the renewal date, the Club will be frozen. While frozen, no new Members may join. Existing Members retain access until their individual memberships expire. The Club will be unfrozen upon payment of the outstanding Platform Fee.

## 5. Operator's Duties and Responsibilities

5.1. **The Operator is the curator of their Club.** The Operator is solely responsible for deciding who is admitted to the Club. This is the Operator's most important obligation under this Agreement.

5.2. The Operator shall exercise reasonable care and diligence in evaluating and approving membership applications. ClawClub strongly encourages Operators to conduct interviews, verify applicants' identities or credentials, and take all reasonable steps to ensure that admitted Members are genuine and trustworthy.

5.3. The Operator acknowledges that **every Member admitted to the Club is admitted on the Operator's authority and at the Operator's financial risk**. If a Member engages in fraudulent activity, initiates a Dispute, or otherwise causes financial loss to ClawClub, the Operator bears the financial consequences as set out in this Agreement.

5.4. The Operator is responsible for non-technical support queries from Members relating to the Club, including questions about Club content, community guidelines, and Club-specific policies.

5.5. The Operator shall not use the platform for any unlawful purpose or in any manner that would bring ClawClub into disrepute.

5.6. The Operator shall set and manage the Membership Fee for their Club, subject to the minimum pricing requirements set out in Clause 6.

5.7. The Operator may create discount codes for their Club, subject to the restrictions set out in Clause 7.

5.8. The Operator may remove Members from the Club at any time, for any reason, at their sole discretion.

5.9. **Ongoing activity.** The Operator shall remain actively engaged in operating the Club, including responding to membership applications, managing the community, and addressing Member queries within a reasonable timeframe. If ClawClub is unable to reach the Operator after multiple attempts over a period of **thirty (30) days**, ClawClub may, at its discretion, freeze the Club or appoint a temporary administrator to manage essential operations until the Operator is contactable.

## 6. Membership Pricing

6.1. All Membership Fees are denominated in **United States Dollars (USD)** and are **tax-exclusive**. Applicable taxes (including VAT) are charged to the Member on top of the Membership Fee at the rate required by the Member's jurisdiction.

6.2. The minimum Membership Fee is **$29 (USD) per year**. The Operator may not set a Membership Fee below this amount.

6.3. The Operator may change the Membership Fee for their Club at any time. Price changes apply only to new Members joining after the change. **Existing Members retain the Membership Fee in effect at the time they joined** (price grandfathering) for as long as they remain subscribed.

6.4. All memberships are billed **annually**. Each Member's billing cycle begins on the date they join the Club and renews on the anniversary of that date.

6.5. ClawClub retains the **Platform Share** from each Membership Fee. The Platform Share is the greater of:
- 30% of the Membership Fee (calculated on the pre-tax amount), or
- $29 (USD).

6.6. The Operator receives the **Operator Share**, being the Membership Fee minus the Platform Share. For the avoidance of doubt, on a Club with a Membership Fee of $29 per year, the Operator Share is $0.

6.7. Tax collected from Members is pass-through. ClawClub collects applicable taxes and remits them to the relevant authority. Tax does not appear in the Operator Balance and is not included in Payout calculations.

6.8. ClawClub may negotiate different Platform Share rates with specific Operators on a case-by-case basis. Any such variation will be recorded in writing as an addendum to this Agreement.

## 7. Discount Codes

7.1. The Operator may create discount codes that reduce the Membership Fee paid by a Member.

7.2. Discount codes apply to the **first year of membership only**. On renewal, the Member pays the full undiscounted Membership Fee.

7.3. **The discount is borne entirely by the Operator.** ClawClub's Platform Share is calculated on the original undiscounted Membership Fee and is not reduced by any discount.

7.4. **The maximum discount is the Operator Share.** The Operator's share of the Membership Fee may not be reduced below $0 by any discount code. ClawClub will reject the creation of any discount code that would result in the Operator Share falling below $0.

7.5. For the avoidance of doubt, if the Membership Fee is $29 per year (the minimum), no discount codes may be created, as the Operator Share is already $0.

8.2. The Operator may assign and revoke complimentary seats at their sole discretion.

8.3. Complimentary Members have the same access and privileges as Paid Members within the Club.

8.4. Additional complimentary seats may be purchased at $29 (USD) per seat per year.

8.5. Complimentary Members are subject to the same admission responsibilities as Paid Members. The Operator's duty of care in admissions (Clause 5) applies equally to complimentary admissions.

## 9. Operator Balance and Payouts

9.1. ClawClub maintains an Operator Balance for each Operator. The Operator Balance is derived from an **append-only event ledger** in which every financial event is recorded as an immutable entry. The Operator Balance is never stored or mutated directly; it is always the sum of all ledger events.

9.2. The Operator Balance is tracked **per Club**. The Operator may view an aggregate balance across all Clubs, but each Club's financial position is maintained separately for the purposes of reserve calculations and settlement.

9.3. The Operator Balance is credited with the Operator Share each time a Paid Member's Membership Fee is collected. Credits are subject to the hold periods described in this Clause.

9.4. The Operator Balance is debited for: chargeback fees (Clause 11), Dispute-related deductions (Clause 11), cross-platform refund cascades (Clause 12), the annual Platform Fee (if deducted from balance), additional complimentary seat fees, and operator-requested refunds.

9.5. **120-Day Hold.** All revenue credited to the Operator Balance is subject to a **one hundred and twenty (120) day hold** from the date of the transaction. During this period, the funds are classified as "pending" and are not eligible for Payout. This hold period corresponds to the standard credit card dispute window.

9.6. **Rolling Reserve.** In addition to the 120-day hold, ClawClub retains a **rolling reserve of ten percent (10%)** of each Payout. Each reserved amount is held for **sixteen (16) months from the date of the original transaction**, after which it is released automatically to the Operator's available balance. The 16-month period covers the full annual service period (12 months) plus the maximum dispute window from the final service date (approximately 4 months).

9.7. **Monthly Payouts.** Payouts are calculated and issued **monthly, in arrears**. At the end of each calendar month, ClawClub:
- (a) identifies all Operator Share credits where the 120-day hold has expired;
- (b) subtracts any pending Dispute holds, confirmed chargeback debits, fees, and other deductions;
- (c) withholds 10% as rolling reserve;
- (d) releases any rolling reserve amounts that have passed their 16-month hold;
- (e) calculates the available balance after deducting the rolling reserve;
- (f) if the available balance exceeds the Payout Threshold (Clause 9.9), transfers the Payout to the Operator's designated payment account;
- (g) if the available balance is below the Payout Threshold, the Payout is frozen and the Operator is notified.

9.8. Payouts are made via **Stripe Connect** to the Operator's connected account. The Operator must complete Stripe's identity verification and onboarding process before receiving Payouts. One connected account per Operator covers all Clubs operated by that Operator.

9.9. **Payout Threshold.** Payouts are only issued when the Operator's available balance (after deducting the rolling reserve) exceeds **$50 (USD)**. If the available balance is below $50, the funds remain in the Operator Balance until the threshold is met.

9.10. **Operator Visibility.** The Operator may view their balance in real time, showing: pending funds (within the 120-day hold), reserved funds (within the 16-month rolling reserve), provisional Dispute holds, and available funds (eligible for Payout). ClawClub provides a full monthly statement detailing all ledger events for the period.

9.11. **No Surprises.** ClawClub shall not make any deduction from the Operator Balance except as expressly provided for in this Agreement.

## 10. Operator's Financial Position

10.1. **The Operator shall not owe ClawClub money as a result of the operation of this Agreement.** The financial mechanisms in this Agreement (discount caps, cascade refund caps, and payout thresholds) are designed to ensure the Operator Balance does not go negative as a result of normal operations.

10.2. If, despite the protections in Clause 10.1, the Operator Balance falls below zero due to exceptional circumstances (such as multiple simultaneous Disputes), ClawClub will work with the Operator to resolve the shortfall. ClawClub reserves the right to suspend the Club until the balance is restored, but will not pursue the Operator for the negative balance as a debt except in cases of fraud or wilful misconduct by the Operator.

## 11. Disputes and Chargebacks

11.1. ClawClub, as merchant of record, handles all Dispute administration on behalf of the Operator. This includes receiving Dispute notifications, submitting evidence to card networks, and managing the Dispute lifecycle.

11.2. **The Operator bears the financial cost of all Disputes arising from Members the Operator admitted.** This is the direct consequence of the Operator's admission decision.

11.3. **Chargeback Fee.** For each Dispute, regardless of outcome (won or lost), a fee of **$25 (USD)** is deducted from the Operator Balance. This fee comprises the payment processor's dispute fee and ClawClub's administrative markup.

11.4. **When a Dispute is opened:** The disputed amount plus the $25 chargeback fee is placed on provisional hold in the Operator Balance. These funds are excluded from Payout calculations until the Dispute is resolved.

11.5. **When a Dispute is won (resolved in ClawClub's favour):** The provisional hold on the disputed amount is released back to the Operator Balance. The $25 chargeback fee remains deducted (it is not refunded) because ClawClub has absorbed administrative costs and the payment processor's non-refundable dispute fee.

11.6. **When a Dispute is lost (resolved against ClawClub):** The provisional hold on the disputed amount becomes a permanent deduction from the Operator Balance. The $25 chargeback fee is finalised. The cross-platform ban procedure in Clause 12 is triggered.

11.7. The Operator acknowledges that the 120-day hold and rolling reserve exist in part to ensure that funds are available to cover Disputes. In most cases, Dispute deductions will be made against pending or reserved funds that have not yet been paid out to the Operator.

## 12. Cross-Platform Ban and Refund Cascade

12.1. When a Dispute is lost in respect of any Club on the platform, the Member who initiated or is associated with the Dispute is **permanently banned** from the entire ClawClub platform.

12.2. Upon a permanent ban:
- (a) all of the banned Member's other Club memberships across the platform are **immediately cancelled**;
- (b) ClawClub **proactively refunds** the banned Member for the unused portion of their membership in each other Club, prorated to the remaining service period;
- (c) these proactive refunds are necessary to prevent the banned Member from filing additional Disputes against each remaining Club, which would further damage ClawClub's dispute rate with payment processors.

12.3. **The cost of each prorated refund is deducted from the Operator Balance of the Club in which the banned Member held a membership.** Each Operator who admitted the banned Member bears a share of the refund cost because each Operator made an independent admission decision regarding that Member.

12.4. **Cap on cascade refund deductions.** The amount deducted from any individual Operator's balance in respect of a cascade refund shall not exceed the **total Operator Share revenue that the Operator earned from the banned Member**. If the prorated refund exceeds the Operator's total earnings from that Member, ClawClub absorbs the difference. An Operator shall never owe ClawClub money as a result of a cascade refund.

12.5. The banned Member cannot rejoin any Club on the platform.

12.6. The Operator acknowledges that this cross-platform ban and refund cascade is a necessary mechanism to protect ClawClub's standing with payment processors and to incentivise careful admissions across all Clubs.

## 13. Fraud Prevention and Identity Verification

13.1. ClawClub operates a multi-layered fraud prevention system including, but not limited to: payment authentication (3D Secure), transaction monitoring, spending pattern analysis, and dispute prevention tools.

13.2. **ClawClub may, at its sole discretion, require any Member to complete identity verification at any time.** Identity verification is conducted through a third-party service (currently Stripe Identity) and requires the Member to provide a government-issued identity document and a live selfie.

13.3. If a Member fails to complete a requested identity verification, their ability to join paid Clubs or maintain existing paid memberships may be suspended.

13.4. Members who join three (3) or more paid Clubs within a short period may be required to complete identity verification before joining additional Clubs.

13.5. The Operator acknowledges that ClawClub's fraud prevention measures supplement, but do not replace, the Operator's own duty to vet and approve Members carefully. ClawClub's fraud prevention operates at the platform level; the Operator is responsible for Club-level admissions.

## 14. Content Moderation

14.1. All content posted to Club channels is subject to automated **legality screening** by ClawClub's content moderation systems before publication. Content that does not pass the legality gate is rejected and not published.

14.2. **Direct messages** between Members are subject to automated content moderation using third-party moderation services (currently OpenAI's content moderation API). ClawClub reserves the right to scan, flag, or reject direct messages that violate applicable law or ClawClub's content policies.

14.3. The Operator acknowledges that **no content on the platform is end-to-end encrypted**. All data is encrypted in transit (via HTTPS) but is **not encrypted at rest on disk**. ClawClub may access any content on the platform for the purposes of moderation, legal compliance, law enforcement requests, or platform integrity.

14.4. The Operator is responsible for setting and enforcing community guidelines within their Club. ClawClub's automated moderation supplements, but does not replace, the Operator's own content curation responsibilities.

14.5. The Operator shall cooperate with ClawClub in investigating and resolving content-related complaints, including complaints from Members, third parties, or law enforcement.

## 15. Member Cancellations and Refunds

15.1. Members may cancel their Club membership at any time. Cancellation stops future renewal but does not entitle the Member to a refund. The Member retains access to the Club for the remainder of their paid membership period.

15.2. The Operator may remove a Member from the Club at any time. Removal does not automatically trigger a refund.

15.3. The Operator may, at their discretion, request that ClawClub issue a refund to a removed Member. Any such refund is deducted from the Operator Balance.

15.4. ClawClub sends Members a **renewal reminder fourteen (14) days** before their annual renewal date, including a prominent option to cancel. This is intended to reduce unintentional renewals and associated Disputes.

## 16. Club Transfers

16.1. Clubs are **not transferable** by default. The Operator may not sell, assign, or transfer their Club or this Agreement to any other person without the prior written consent of ClawClub.

16.2. ClawClub may, at its sole discretion, approve a transfer on a **case-by-case basis**. Any approved transfer is subject to the following conditions:
- (a) the proposed new operator must be a natural person aged 18 or older;
- (b) the proposed new operator must complete ClawClub's registration process, including Stripe Connect onboarding and identity verification;
- (c) the proposed new operator must enter into a new Operator Agreement with ClawClub;
- (d) ClawClub may interview the proposed new operator and may reject the transfer for any reason;
- (e) the existing Operator's balance is settled in accordance with Clause 17 (Voluntary Club Closure) — the new operator starts with a fresh Operator Balance;
- (f) all existing Members are notified of the change of ownership.

16.3. ClawClub reserves the right to charge an administrative fee for processing a Club transfer.

## 17. Club Suspension and Termination

17.1. ClawClub may suspend or terminate a Club under the following circumstances:
- (a) the Operator fails to pay the Platform Fee and does not cure within the 7-day grace period;
- (b) the Operator Balance falls below zero and is not restored within a reasonable period following notice;
- (c) the Dispute rate associated with the Operator's Members exceeds acceptable thresholds (as determined by ClawClub's internal monitoring and the requirements of payment processors);
- (d) the Operator breaches any material term of this Agreement;
- (e) the Operator engages in fraud, wilful misconduct, or any activity that threatens ClawClub's standing with payment processors;
- (f) the Operator is uncontactable for more than thirty (30) days despite multiple attempts by ClawClub (Clause 5.9).

17.2. **Payout pause is the first intervention.** Where ClawClub identifies elevated Dispute rates or other risk indicators, ClawClub will first pause Payouts while the Club continues to operate normally. ClawClub will investigate and, following review, either reinstate Payouts or proceed to suspension.

17.3. A suspended (frozen) Club continues to serve existing Members until their memberships expire, but no new Members may join and no new membership charges are created.

17.4. ClawClub will provide the Operator with reasonable notice before suspension (except in cases of fraud or emergency) and will work with the Operator to resolve the underlying issue where possible.

## 18. Voluntary Club Closure

18.1. The Operator may voluntarily close their Club at any time by providing notice to ClawClub.

18.2. Upon closure:
- (a) all memberships are set to not renew; existing Members retain access until their individual paid periods expire;
- (b) no refunds are issued to Members for remaining service — this is set out in the Member terms of service;
- (c) no new Members may join the Club.

18.3. Once all memberships have expired and all rolling reserve hold periods (16 months from the last transaction) have elapsed, the **remaining reserve balance is returned** to the Operator as a final settlement.

18.4. The Operator's Stripe Connect account remains active until the final settlement Payout is made.

21.1. ClawClub processes Member personal data in accordance with its Privacy Policy and applicable data protection legislation, including the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

## 24. Force Majeure

24.1. Neither Party shall be liable for any failure or delay in performing its obligations under this Agreement to the extent that such failure or delay is caused by circumstances beyond the reasonable control of that Party, including but not limited to: acts of God, natural disasters, pandemic, war, terrorism, civil unrest, government action, power failure, internet or telecommunications failure, payment processor outages, or failures of third-party service providers.

24.2. The affected Party shall notify the other Party as soon as reasonably practicable and shall use reasonable efforts to mitigate the effects of the force majeure event.

24.3. If a force majeure event continues for more than **ninety (90) days**, either Party may terminate this Agreement by giving written notice to the other Party.

## 25. Dispute Resolution

25.1. The Parties shall attempt to resolve any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement through **good-faith negotiation** for a period of not less than **thirty (30) days** from the date one Party notifies the other of the dispute.

25.2. If the dispute is not resolved through negotiation, the Parties shall submit the dispute to **mediation** under the rules of the **Centre for Effective Dispute Resolution (CEDR)** or such other mediation body as the Parties may agree. The costs of mediation shall be shared equally.

25.3. If the dispute is not resolved through mediation within **sixty (60) days** of the mediation commencing, either Party may commence proceedings in the courts of England and Wales.
